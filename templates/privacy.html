1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service. This includes:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used solely to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the Service. We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

Expense records are retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon deletion, your records will be permanently removed within 30 days.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Spendly account.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Fonts — used to load typefaces. Google may log your IP address when fonts are fetched.
  • Hosting provider — our servers are managed by a cloud hosting provider that processes data on our behalf under a data processing agreement.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics brokers, or social media platforms. No third party receives your expense data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You may also object to or restrict certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions about how your data is handled, please contact us at support@spendly.app. We will respond within 30 days.
